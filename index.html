About Us

Thierry Feuz, born in 1968 in Vienna, Austria, is a Swiss-Austrian contemporary artist known for his vibrant and dynamic abstract works. His art is characterized by a fusion of scientific curiosity, surrealism, and an explosive use of color, creating an immersive visual experience that blurs the boundaries between nature, technology, and imagination.
